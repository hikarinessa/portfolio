How I built it with AI The point of Onyx wasn't the editor — it was proving how fast and reliable AI-assisted development has become. The design was the easy part: replicate the custom Obsidian workflows I already live in, in a lighter, faster app. The real work was architecting the AI collaboration itself — building robust, repeatable ways to hand work to Claude Code and trust what comes back. Onyx is the demonstration: serious dev is more accessible now than most people realise.
